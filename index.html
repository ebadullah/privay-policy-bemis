The BEMIS is an education management information system. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, protected, and managed when users access and use the BEMIS mobile and web applications. 1. Information We Collect Personal Information: User name, designation, and official contact information Login credentials for authorized system access Educational Data: Student attendance, assessment, and behavior data School, teacher, and classroom information Monitoring, observation, and intervention records Device & Usage Information: Device type, OS, app version System usage logs for performance and troubleshooting Location Information: Location data may be collected where required for GIS mapping, school verification, and monitoring 2. How We Use Information Monitoring school performance and attendance Identifying students at risk of dropout Education planning and policy decisions Dashboards, reports, and analytics System performance and reliability improvements 3. Data Sharing & Disclosure No sale or commercial sharing of data Access limited to authorized government officials Shared only for official or legal purposes 4. Data Security Secure authentication Role-based access control Encrypted data transmission Restricted access to sensitive data 5. Data Retention Data is retained only as required under government record management and legal policies 6. User Responsibilities Keep login credentials confidential Ensure accuracy of submitted data Use the system strictly for official purposes 7. Children's Privacy BEMIS does not directly collect data from children Student data is managed only by authorized officials 8. Application Permissions Camera: Document capture Location: GIS verification Storage: Upload/download official files Internet: Secure data synchronization 9. Policy Updates This policy may be updated periodically and becomes effective immediately upon publication 10. Contact Information Email: support@bemisportal.com Website: http://bemisportal.com
